Home is central to human life
At The Giving Tree, we care for adoptive families by helping them create joyful homes.

What is Happy Family?

A happy family is one where members feel emotionally secure, valued, and connected. In adoptive families, this means creating a safe space where open communication and mutual respect thrive.

"A happy family is one where all family members feel loved and safe. It means having mostly positive interactions and communication with each other." – Dr. Lucy Russell

Emotional security, shared routines, and a healthy balance between individuality and togetherness are key to nurturing happiness in any home. For adoptive families, these elements are especially crucial in supporting the unique identity and well-being of adoptees.

For Successful Adoptive Family

Adoption is a life-changing decision that impacts both parents and children in profound ways. While it offers the promise of love and stability, it also presents unique challenges, including identity struggles, trauma, and genetic influences on relationships.

A successful adoptive family is not defined by mere legal bonds but by emotional security, communication, and an awareness of the adoptee’s unique needs.
